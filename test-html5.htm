Your guide to HTML 4

So, you want to learn HTML 4. The first thing you need to know is that element usage is inconsistent.

Using headings

Dividing up your page

So your page is not just a mass of words, you need headings. You should insert them in-between logical sections of paragraphs.

How do I deal with multiple-level headings?

Sometimes, you'll want a 'subheading' to your headings. Things like:

Dr. Strangelove, Or: How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb
For that, just use more than one heading level: 
    <h1>Dr. Strangelove</h1>
      <h2>Or: How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb</h2>
